Freelance Work and Tutoring

Private Tutoring

I have been a private tutor since April 2021, and am constantly taking new bookings! Send me an email if you would be interested in setting up some sessions. Trial lessons are half price, and rate is to be agreed subject to level, session length, and number of sessions.

I am:

  • Fully DBS Checked
  • Able to take bookings as registered self-employed
  • Experienced with students from age 3 to 18
  • Recommended for my engaging online lessons with a variety of age groups
  • Flexible in my schedule since this is my primary occupation at the moment

I have been class-based tutoring since 2014 as a volunteer (and then employed) class assistant at Mayfield School's SPARK camps, as well as at Mayfield Primary School, and as an EFL (and French second language) tutor in Cambodia. I have been one-to-one tutoring students online aged from Key Stage 2 to A-Level since 2021 with Witherow Brooke Tuition, and alongside this I have helped several prospective applicants across a range of subjects (predominantly Linguistics, but also Music, History, and English Literature) and a variety of colleges (predominantly King's, but also Girton, Pembroke, and St John's). Every student (private or informal) that I have helped has gained an offer (and a confirmed place after A-Levels) from their first-choice college.
